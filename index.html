Introduction

The platform advances UN SDG 4 by providing an accessible hub for learners, teachers, parents and stakeholders. It tackles gaps in information, resources and digital access with an open, inclusive design.

Objectives

  • Create a user-friendly platform focused on SDG 4.
  • Offer free learning materials and digital skills training.
  • Connect educators, learners and policymakers.
  • Raise awareness on inclusive education.

Target Audience

  • Refugees & vulnerable learners
  • Teachers & educators
  • Parents & guardians
  • NGOs & policymakers

Outcomes

  • Increased access to quality resources
  • Improved digital literacy
  • Stronger learning networks
  • Direct contribution to SDG 4
